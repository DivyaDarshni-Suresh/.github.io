Leveraging AI and UX to Improve Learning and Education for Children with Autism

A primary research attempt to combine UX and AI for the betterment of education of autistic children with the guidance of Professor Bart Selman, a renowned expret in the field of Artificial Intelligence. For this project, I did an extensive study on various research papers to understand what is the need and what is the concern of the hour based on which I developed a figma prototype and a machine learning model with different models. An accuarcy of 99% was obtained with the ensemble method.
